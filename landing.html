What are renewable fuel sources?

  • Renewable fuel are enrgy sources that are recyclable, and more importantly emite no carbon emissions.
  • You may be suprised to find out what is considerd net possitive and what is not.
  • net negative is when a fuel source contributes more carbon emissions into the enviroment
  • net positive is when the fuel source produces zero carbon emissions using renewable resources.
  • net neutral means that the fuel source may come from non-renewables, but emites no carbon emissions
