The Birth of Industrial Revolution

Technology is booming and life in the modern era is changing. This is where or rather when we will begin. According to economists, an "Idustrial Revollution" is defined by a burst of economic growth attributed to new ways of communication, transportation, and power sources. Although many technologies are of great importance, we will focus on these three major developments.
